About This Blog

For some time now, I have always wanted to start my own blog. I draw my inspiration from a blog I started following recently. In James's blog, he lists several reasons why he started his own blog and mine are similar:

  • To keep a record of the things I do during college
  • To reflect upon how I develop as a person and as a friend to others (Somewhat similar to the first reason)
  • To improve my writing

Much of the content here will contain personal information. But, I will not disclose information that should be kept private. I'll keep that to myself. In the entries below, you will not learn much about the intimate details of my life. Nor should you find anything inappropriate - I don't think I do anything reckless. Instead, you might encounter a log of what I've done that day. My friends have said that reading my initial entries resembles reading a diary without the appropriateness of doing so. Perhaps, you will realize the same feeling. Anyways, feel free to read the articles below. I hope that what you read below will match how you find me in person!
